## PAT-ART-DRAPERY-001 — Folds Come From Forces
IF drawing cloth folds  
THEN identify the force first: hanging, tension, compression, twist, shear.  
PASS: folds point to support/tension lines.  
FAIL: random zig-zags.

## PAT-ART-DRAPERY-002 — Anchor Points Create Fold Families
IF cloth touches body/clip/hand/ground  
THEN mark anchors and let fold families radiate from them.  
PASS: folds feel organized and believable.  
FAIL: even spacing, no cause.

## PAT-ART-DRAPERY-003 — Material Changes Fold Language
IF material changes (stiff, soft, heavy)  
THEN change fold frequency + sharpness + amplitude accordingly.  
PASS: viewer can “feel” the fabric weight.  
FAIL: denim folds like silk.

## PAT-ART-DRAPERY-004 — Simplify at Distance
IF the subject is not the focal point  
THEN reduce folds to a few major lines and value masses.  
PASS: cloth supports the read, doesn’t steal focus.  
FAIL: noisy micro-folds everywhere.
