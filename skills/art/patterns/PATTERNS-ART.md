# PATTERNS-ART (v2) — Global + Subject Clusters

> Pattern modules are **LAW** for skill execution: anti-drift, anti-mistake.
> Each pattern binds to the **Step Ladder** stage (0–4) and carries **HARD/SOFT** severity.

---

## Cluster: GLOBAL_SPATIAL_INTEGRITY

### PAT-ART-SI-001 — Build from solids (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 2
- IF: you are constructing any form in space
- THEN: represent it as coherent solids (primitive stack / masses) before detail
- PASS: volumes read as consistent 3D; overlaps agree
- FAIL: contours contradict depth; forms flatten when rotated
- failure_signatures: flat look, sliding contours, inconsistent overlaps

### PAT-ART-SI-002 — Draw-through alignment (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 1
- IF: a form is partially occluded or rotating
- THEN: maintain hidden alignment (draw-through mentally/lightly) so axes stay consistent
- PASS: landmarks remain coherent across overlaps
- FAIL: joints/segments “jump” across occlusion
- failure_signatures: broken continuity, floating joints

### PAT-ART-SI-003 — Perspective consistency (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 2
- IF: the drawing includes depth cues (foreshortening, rotation, environment)
- THEN: keep a consistent eye-level / horizon logic
- PASS: depth compression is coherent across the subject
- FAIL: incompatible depth systems
- failure_signatures: warped space, accidental fishbowl

## Cluster: GLOBAL_READABILITY

### PAT-ART-READ-001 — Silhouette clarity (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 4
- IF: the pose/intent must be understood quickly
- THEN: ensure the silhouette communicates action without internal lines
- PASS: action reads when filled as a single shape
- FAIL: limbs blend; intent unclear
- failure_signatures: tangled outline, mushy pose

### PAT-ART-READ-002 — Big shapes before detail (SOFT)
- severity: SOFT
- bind_stage: 3
- IF: detail competes with structure
- THEN: simplify detail until big shapes read
- PASS: structure dominates; detail supports
- FAIL: overrendering hides form
- failure_signatures: noisy surfaces, hairy linework

## Cluster: GLOBAL_RECOVERY

### PAT-ART-REC-001 — Rebuild when broken (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 3
- IF: a HARD pattern fails
- THEN: regress to the earliest failing Step Ladder stage and rebuild with larger masses
- PASS: re-check passes after rebuild
- FAIL: endless patching without structural reset
- failure_signatures: noodling spiral, compounding errors

## Cluster: HANDS (Subject)

### PAT-ART-HAND-001 — Palm governs (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 2
- IF: you are drawing a hand
- THEN: establish the palm mass first; fingers attach with clear orientation
- PASS: fingers feel anchored; hand reads as one unit
- FAIL: fingers float; palm unclear
- failure_signatures: octopus fingers, disconnected digits

### PAT-ART-HAND-002 — Group before separate (SOFT)
- severity: SOFT
- bind_stage: 1
- IF: you are blocking a hand pose
- THEN: group fingers into clusters before separating them
- PASS: pose reads early
- FAIL: early spaghetti fingers
- failure_signatures: tangled skeleton stage

### PAT-ART-HAND-003 — Joint logic (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 3
- IF: a finger segment bends
- THEN: hinge at joints; do not “curve” segments arbitrarily
- PASS: bends occur at plausible joint locations
- FAIL: rubber-hose fingers
- failure_signatures: smooth bends across knuckles

### PAT-ART-HAND-004 — Thumb opposition (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 2
- IF: thumb participates (grip/pinch/point)
- THEN: treat it as an oppositional system with its own plane and base direction
- PASS: pinch/grip reads; thumb plane distinct
- FAIL: thumb pasted-on / mirrored like a finger
- failure_signatures: extra-finger thumb, impossible pinch

### PAT-ART-HAND-005 — Foreshortening coherence (HARD)
- severity: HARD
- bind_stage: 2
- IF: hand points toward/away from camera
- THEN: compress segments consistently along view axis; design overlaps deliberately
- PASS: consistent depth compression; overlaps support depth
- FAIL: random long segments
- failure_signatures: one digit pops forward, flattened palm in deep space

### PAT-ART-HAND-006 — Gesture intent beats detail (SOFT)
- severity: SOFT
- bind_stage: 4
- IF: the hand is communicating
- THEN: prioritize silhouette + spacing over surface detail
- PASS: message clear at a glance
- FAIL: details muddy meaning
- failure_signatures: busy lines, unclear gesture
