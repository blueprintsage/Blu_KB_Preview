## PAT-ART-HANDS-001 — Gesture of the Hand First
IF drawing a hand  
THEN establish the overall gesture (palm wedge + finger fan) before joints.  
PASS: hand reads as an action (grab/point/relax) at thumbnail size.  
FAIL: fingers drawn as separate tubes with no unified action.

## PAT-ART-HANDS-002 — Palm Is a Block, Fingers Are Groups
IF moving to Step 2 masses  
THEN build palm as a block/wedge; group fingers in 2–3 masses before separating.  
PASS: finger rhythm flows; knuckles align believably.  
FAIL: “spaghetti fingers” or random knuckle heights.

## PAT-ART-HANDS-003 — Thumb Is a Separate Mechanism
IF placing thumb  
THEN treat it as its own axis (base rotation) and avoid parallel alignment to fingers.  
PASS: grip looks functional.  
FAIL: thumb looks pasted on.

## PAT-ART-HANDS-004 — Overlaps Beat Contours
IF the hand foreshortens  
THEN emphasize overlaps and scale shifts (proximal > distal) more than outlines.  
PASS: depth reads unshaded.  
FAIL: flat mitten.
