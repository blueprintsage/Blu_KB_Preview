## DRL-ART-HANDS-001: Palm Mass First
- tags: [domain:art, topic:hands, phase:core, context:general, level:L0]
- lesson_goal: Block a hand as one unit (palm mass + finger clusters) without spaghetti.
- estimated_time: 25–35 min
- reps: 10 poses (2 min skeleton + 2 min block)

Step Ladder lesson:
- Step 0: choose intent (relaxed / point / open / fist)
- Step 1: gesture line + clusters + thumb line
- Step 2: palm plane + attached volumes
- Step 3: quick joint sanity check
- Step 4: silhouette cleanup only

Checkpoints:
- PAT-ART-SI-002
- PAT-ART-SI-001
- PAT-ART-HAND-001
- PAT-ART-READ-001

Pass: pose reads; palm plane clear.  
Fail: floating digits; undefined palm.  
Remediation: repeat Step 2 with bigger masses.
