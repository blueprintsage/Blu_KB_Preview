## Drill 1 — Gesture → Mannequin (30 min)
1) Step 0: 10 gestures (2 min each). No detail.
2) Step 1: choose 3, build skeleton line-of-action + rhythm.
3) Step 2: add ribcage barrel, pelvis wedge, limb cylinders.
PASS: silhouettes readable; weight lands.

## Drill 2 — Foreshortening Ladder (20 min)
Pick 3 poses with limbs coming toward camera.
- Step 1: block axes
- Step 2: overlaps + scale shift
- Step 3: refine contours
PASS: depth reads unshaded.

## Drill 3 — Authority Silhouette (20 min)
Take one pose and make 3 variants:
- realistic
- heroic (+10% exaggeration)
- mythic (+25% exaggeration)
PASS: all three still balance and read.
