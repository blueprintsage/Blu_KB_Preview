## Drill 1 — 12 Torso Constructions (30–40 min)
Goal: lock the two-mass torso.
1) Step 0: 12 tiny torso thumbnails (tilt/twist/arch).
2) Step 1: ribcage + pelvis solids, spine axis.
3) Step 2: add simple wrap rhythms (no detail).
PASS: each reads at thumbnail.

## Drill 2 — Torque Set (20–30 min)
Draw 6 torsos twisting:
- show ribcage vs pelvis counter-rotation clearly.
PASS: twist feels powered without rendering.

## Drill 3 — Compression/Expansion Set (20–30 min)
Draw 6 side-bends:
- mark compression side (stacked) vs stretch side (simplified).
PASS: squash/stretch readable.

## Drill 4 — Abs on a Barrel (15–25 min)
Draw 6 torsos in foreshortening:
- place abdomen forms following curvature and perspective.
PASS: no “brick abs” on flat plane.

## Drill 5 — Production Ladder Snapshot (10 min)
Pick ONE torso and do a 5-frame ladder:
- Step 0 → Step 4
PASS: you can show the evidence chain quickly.
