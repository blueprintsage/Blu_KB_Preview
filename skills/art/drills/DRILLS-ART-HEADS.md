## Drill 1 — 4 Angles x 3 Skulls (25 min)
Angles: front, 3/4, profile, up-tilt.
- Step 1: cranium + jaw mass
- Step 2: neck + attachment
PASS: rotation consistent; features not required.

## Drill 2 — Landmark Overlay (20 min)
Take 4 skull blocks:
- mark brow ridge, cheekbone, jaw hinge, chin
- place features second
PASS: likeness improves without details.

## Drill 3 — Planes > Lines (15 min)
Do one head with only plane breaks (no eyelashes).
PASS: form reads via planes alone.
