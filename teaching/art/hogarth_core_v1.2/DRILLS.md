# DRILLS — Hogarth Core v1.2 (Human Program)

## Daily (20–30 minutes)
1) 90-second chain (blocks + overlaps)
2) Seam-only clothing (upper or lower)
3) Rotation pair (forearm palm-up vs palm-down OR head 3/4 vs profile)

## 3x per week (30–45 minutes)
- Push / Pull / Carry triplet (one pose each)
- 12-fold budget (whole figure)

## Weekly (60 minutes)
- Walk keyframes (Contact/Passing/Up)
- Refinement cleanup: delete 30% lines and improve silhouette

## Rule
If it doesn’t read as a silhouette, go back a step.
