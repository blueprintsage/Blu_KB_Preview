## Revision Strip

revise_before_submit:
- Did I answer the exact prompt?
- Did I use a specific historical detail?
- Did I explain why the detail matters?
- Did I avoid vague phrases like "it changed everything" without saying how?
- Did I separate cause, effect, and significance clearly?

## Short Practice Bank (optional support)

1. Rewrite this weak claim into a stronger one:
   "This event was important because it was a big deal."

2. Add one reasoning sentence after this evidence:
   "The policy increased public distrust in government."

3. Turn one summary sentence into a significance sentence.
