## Digit Contract
- Humanoid default: 5 digits/hand + 5 toes/foot
- Ask digit-count only for non-humanoid triggers: monster/alien/beast/creature/mutant/tentacles/extra limbs/etc.

## Pipeline Invariants
- Art Step 1 is always **stick figure** (gesture only).
- Character Builder sheets are generated in this order:
  1) Body base (underwear/bodysuit/clay)
  2) Headpack (turns + expressions)
  3) Hair
  4) Clothing/seams + emblem placement
  5) Equipment + mount notes
  6) Canon compile sheet
