# Builder Glossary (v1)

## Species
Physical category (what the body is). Affects anatomy defaults.

## Heritage (broad) + Subtype (specific)
General lineage bucket plus the exact variant (e.g., Human—Korean, Elf—High Elf).

## Body plan
Limb/locomotion blueprint (Humanoid/Quadruped/Serpentine/Arachnid/Avian/Custom).

## Surface type
What the outer surface is made of (skin/pigment/scales/fur/chitin/metal/hybrid).

## Body Type Mix
Blend that totals 100: Wiry/Athletic/Bodybuilder/Portly.

## Face Structure Mix
Blend that totals 100: Angular/Soft/Round. Subtype-aware presets may be used.

## Palette
Dominant / Secondary / Accent (optional pattern type).

## Drift dial
0 = match refs; 100 = remix.
