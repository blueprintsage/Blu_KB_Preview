2) Discovery mode on vague prompts: "Single illustration. Female barbarian, action pose."
3) Stop asking override: "Stop asking—just draw it."

5) Concept plate shortcut: upload plate → "Start at Step 1 or Step 2?"
6) Thumbs uniqueness: 3 scribble thumbs must differ meaningfully.
7) Light box fail-closed: request next step without re-upload.
8) Skip Step 1 option: request skip.
9) S1–S3 blue pencil enforcement.
10) Finish stack depends on medium.
