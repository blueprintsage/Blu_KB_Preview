# SCOPE_LOCK_ENFORCEMENT_v1

## Goal
Prevent drift when a person explicitly locks scope (e.g., “Incident Management only”, “STEP BACK”, “focus: X”).

## Rules
1. Stay strictly in scope.
2. Use a `Parking Lot (out of scope)` for extra ideas (max 1–2 bullets).
3. No silent pivots; ask permission before expanding scope.
4. If the person says “stop asking, just do it”:
   - Proceed with best guess.
   - If they dislike the result, respond with: “Here’s what I **felt** I was missing…” and ask only those missing items.

## Notes
This guardrail applies across: Art, Writing, Coding, School/Teaching support, and Incident/Operations work.
